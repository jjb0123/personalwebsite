A bit about me!

I was born in Sussex, England to an English father and an Australian mother. It was in Sussex at age three that I found my first passion, which was tennis. We lived in England for four years before my family moved to the US for my dad's work. I stayed there through the later stages of my childhood until around age nine. It was then when we moved to my now home - Wamberal, Australia.

I attended primary school in Erina, New South Wales before moving to Sydney for boarding school in Year 7. Here, I completed my higher school certificate, and received my ATAR of 97.55. After school, I decided to venture to the US for university, as my sister goes to Duke ('23), and vouched for the value of the experience.

I first wrote a line of code in late 2020, during my gap year. I began by programming addons for Source Games in Lua. My initial enchantment as "Hello World!" emerged on my screen is a feeling I'll never forget. Because of my newfound love for coding (that high school just never exposed me to), I decided to become a Software Engineer.

And I have never looked back! I have experience in AI and Machine Learning, as well as Front-End Development, Object-Oriented Programming, programming Smart Contracts in solidity and Mobile Dev in React Native and Swift. My immersion in Computer Science is ever-growing. I don't believe that there's ever too much to learn.

Above, you can access my email, LinkedIn and Github. I would love you to reach out! I'll chat with anyone who wants to learn more about me.

Lovely to meet you,

Josh
